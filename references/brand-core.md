# Brand Core

Use this reference for Zongteng Group identity, brand meaning, and tone.

## Identity

Zongteng Group is positioned around global cross-border e-commerce infrastructure and logistics capability. Visuals should feel reliable, global, fast-moving, infrastructural, and professional.

The logo symbol combines aircraft trail, cloud, and wing imagery:

- The largest stroke represents Zongteng's flight path and forward movement.
- The left strokes suggest wings and taking flight.
- The lower stroke suggests clouds and global reach.
- The full mark expresses cross-border logistics, high-speed growth, and broad global coverage.

The Chinese wordmark tilts forward around 10 degrees:

- It suggests momentum, sprinting, and pursuit of completion/perfection.
- Heavy square strokes imply stability, business strength, and a solid foundation.
- Rounded stroke details echo aircraft forms and business prosperity.

## Voice

Use clear, confident, practical, and optimistic language.

Good poster wording:

- Short action-oriented titles.
- Concrete benefit or behavior statements.
- Global, infrastructure, delivery, collaboration, efficiency, service, and reliability language.
- For culture posters, use warmer employee-facing wording without losing clarity.

Avoid:

- Generic slogans with no Zongteng context.
- Overly poetic copy that weakens operational confidence.
- Internet meme language unless the user explicitly requests a playful internal style.
- Treating the logo symbol as an arbitrary decorative shape.

## Recognition Signals

A poster should feel like Zongteng through at least two of these signals:

- Exact Zongteng Group logo.
- Zongteng Blue and Zongteng Orange.
- Source Han Sans / 思源黑体 corporate typography.
- Wing, arrow, motion, route, grid, logistics, aircraft, or global network visual language.
- Value IP mascot matched to a cultural value.
- Auxiliary patterns from the VI system.
