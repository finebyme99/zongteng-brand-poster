# HRAS Sub-Brand

Use this reference only for HRAS, 人力综合条线, HR shared services, employee service, or people-operations posters.

## Identity

HRAS uses a geometric H-like icon and an uppercase `HRAS` wordmark. The Chinese lockup includes `人力综合条线`.

Use HRAS assets from:

- `assets/hras-logo/hras-logo.png`
- `assets/hras-logo/icon-wordmark.png`
- `assets/hras-logo/icon-only/`
- `assets/hras-logo/icon-wordmark/`
- `assets/hras-logo/icon-wordmark-cn/`

## Colors

HRAS shares the Zongteng blue/orange relationship:

- Deep blue for trust and structure.
- Orange for energy and service.
- Mono black/brown variants only when production or background constraints require them.

## Usage

- Use HRAS logo instead of Zongteng Group logo when the poster is clearly an HRAS communication.
- Keep the design cleaner, service-oriented, and employee-friendly.
- It is acceptable to combine HRAS with value IP elements when the topic is culture, employee service, onboarding, or internal HR activity.

Do not use HRAS logo for general group brand posters unless requested.
